Joy Heyer

Joy Heyer is the author and illustrator of the picture book Duck, Duck, Moose. She lives in Virginia with her husband, 3 of her children, and a dog. Her oldest is married with 4 children of her own making Joy a Grandma. She enjoys being goofy, singing out loud, and doing almost anything to embarrass her children. Fortunately, they are okay with it and sometimes join in. To see more of Joy's illustrations, visit her website, joyheyer.com, or follow her on instagram: @joy_illustrated.
