Meet the Characters

Duck and Moose

With his best friend gone for winter, Duck is lonely and has no one to play with. Duck is curious and friendly, but also stubborn.

Moose is big and friendly. He loves to tromp around and play games with other animals!

Pig

Pig loves to frollick in the mud, and he's not afraid to get his hands dirty! Ever cheerful and smiley, Pig is always welcome to play games with other animals!
